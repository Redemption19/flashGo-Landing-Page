How The App Works?

User Registration/Login

Users download the app from an app store and register for an account. They provide basic information such as name, email, and phone number, and set up a payment method.

Finding a Ride

When users need a ride, they open the app and enter their pickup location and destination. The app then displays available taxis and motorcycles nearby.

Choosing a Vehicle

Users can choose between a taxi or a motorcycle based on their preference, urgency, and other factors such as traffic conditions or weather.

Real-Time Tracking

After selecting a vehicle, users can track the driver's location in real-time on a map within the app. They receive updates on the estimated time of arrival (ETA) and can communicate with the driver if needed.

Booking and Confirmation

Once users confirm their ride, the app sends a notification to the driver with the pickup location. The driver can either accept or reject the ride based on their availability.

Driver Arrival

When the driver arrives at the pickup location, the user receives a notification. They can then proceed to the vehicle for the ride.

Payment and Ratings

After completing the ride, users have the option to pay through the app using their preferred payment method. They can also rate the driver and provide feedback on their experience.

History and Records

Users can access their ride history within the app, including details such as past trips, payment receipts, and driver ratings.

Frequently Asked Questions

FlashGo is a ride-hailing application that connects passengers with drivers for convenient transportation services.

FlashGo works by allowing users to request rides through the mobile app. Drivers nearby receive these requests and can choose to accept and fulfill them. Once a driver accepts a ride request, the passenger is notified, and they can track the driver's location until they reach their destination.

The major challenges of current transportation include issues such as traffic congestion, inefficient public transportation systems, and limited accessibility in remote areas.

FlashGo aims to address transportation challenges by providing a convenient, efficient, and accessible ride-hailing service. By connecting passengers with nearby drivers, FlashGo helps reduce traffic congestion, provide reliable transportation options, and improve accessibility, especially in areas with limited public transportation.

To become a part of FlashGo, you can download the mobile app from the App Store or Google Play Store and sign up either as a passenger or a driver.

FlashGo is constantly expanding to new cities. To check if FlashGo is available in your city, simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store and enter your location. If FlashGo is available, you'll be able to request rides right away.
